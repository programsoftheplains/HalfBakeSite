The Good News

We are not laywers...

So it's hard to say if we're doing this right

BUT! We're gonna try.

So we sort of have a copyright on the zine (we think), just to protect the IP rights of each piece. The contents themselves are still owned by the people who made them, but the assembled work itself constitutes like... a thing. It took a lot of mental anguish for everyone involved to photograph/write/collage/bang-two-mallets-on-a-computer and assemble this thing, and we want to respect the due ownership that comes along with that. That being said, we are totally cool with you reprinting and sharing our volumes under these conditions:

1: Print the whole volume and keep the credits in there!

2: Just shoot us an email to let us know! We thrive on the positive attention.

That's it! Thanks in advance!
